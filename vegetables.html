Vegetables

Our Vegetable Sources

Dambulla Dedicated Economic Center

The Dambulla Dedicated Economic Center is one of Sri Lanka's largest wholesale markets for vegetables and fruits. Located in the heart of the island, this bustling hub operates 24/7, ensuring a constant supply of fresh produce. Farmers from all over the country bring their harvests here, where it is meticulously sorted and distributed to retailers. This center is renowned for its wide variety of vegetables, from common staples like tomatoes and potatoes to exotic local varieties such as bitter gourd and winged beans. By sourcing from Dambulla, we guarantee that our vegetables are fresh, diverse, and support local agriculture.

Nuwara Eliya Farms

Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as "Little England," is famous for its cool climate and fertile soil, making it an ideal region for cultivating high-quality vegetables. The farms in this area are known for producing crisp and flavorful vegetables, including lettuce, carrots, leeks, and radishes. These farms employ sustainable farming practices, ensuring that the vegetables are grown without harmful pesticides, preserving their natural goodness. By partnering with Nuwara Eliya farms, we bring you farm-fresh produce that is as nutritious as it is delicious.
